# Monkey & River

**Software development consultancy building digital products that matter.**

Monkey & River is a South African software development consultancy. We partner with organisations across healthcare, enterprise, education, and connectivity to design, build, and maintain custom software products - from mobile apps and web platforms to complex distributed systems.

## What We Do

We operate as embedded development teams for our clients, providing end-to-end software delivery under a Team as a Service model. Our work spans the full product lifecycle: architecture, development, deployment, and long-term maintenance. We build for web, mobile, and cloud - choosing the right technology for the problem rather than forcing a single stack.

## Industries We Work In

- **Healthcare** - Patient portals, health management platforms, and medical data systems
- **Enterprise** - Business platforms, management tools, and operational software
- **Education** - School management apps, learning tools, and student engagement platforms
- **Connectivity and IoT** - Device management platforms and network infrastructure

## Our Stack

We work across a broad range of technologies depending on what each project needs, including Go, TypeScript, Angular, Flutter, Dart, C#/.NET, Python, Java, and PHP. We build for web, iOS, and Android.
