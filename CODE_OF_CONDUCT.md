# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Monkey & River is committed to maintaining a professional, respectful, and inclusive environment across all projects and repositories in this organisation. Everyone who contributes, reviews, or participates in discussions is expected to uphold this standard.

## Expected Behaviour

- Communicate professionally and respectfully
- Give and receive constructive feedback without personal criticism
- Acknowledge different perspectives and experience levels
- Focus on what is best for the project and the team
- Assume good intent before escalating disagreements

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Dismissing or belittling contributions without explanation
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all repositories under the Monkey-River GitHub organisation, including issues, pull requests, code review comments, and any other project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this code of conduct, please raise it with a Monkey & River team lead or contact us at **info@monkeyandriver.com**. All reports will be handled with discretion.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in a warning, removal of contributions, or removal from the organisation at the discretion of Monkey & River leadership.
